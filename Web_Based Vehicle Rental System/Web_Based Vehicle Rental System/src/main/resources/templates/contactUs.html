Frequently Asked Questions

What documents do I need to rent a vehicle?

You'll need a valid driver's license, a credit card in your name, and proof of insurance. International renters may need additional documentation.

What is your cancellation policy?

You can cancel your reservation up to 24 hours before your scheduled pickup time without any charges. Later cancellations may incur a fee.

Do you offer long-term rentals?

Yes, we offer special rates for long-term rentals. Contact us directly for customized quotes for rentals longer than 30 days.

What happens if I return the vehicle late?

We allow a 59-minute grace period. After that, additional charges will apply based on our hourly rates.
